📚 Word Guide for Explorers

Weather Types

☀️

Sunny

The sun is big and bright. No clouds!

☁️

Cloudy

The sky is full of white or grey clouds.

🌫️

Foggy

Clouds are on the ground! It's hard to see.

❄️

Icy

Very, very cold! Water turns into hard ice.

How often? (Frequency)

100%

Always

It happens every single day.

50%

Sometimes

It happens half of the time.

0%

Never

It is impossible! No way!

📖 Grammar Guide: CAN · MUST · MUSTN'T

✅

CAN

It is possible / allowed

"You can visit the church."
→ It is possible to do this.

⚠️

MUST

It is necessary / obligatory

"You must follow the path."
→ It is very important. Do it!

🚫

MUSTN'T

It is not allowed / forbidden

"You mustn't touch the art."
→ Don't do this! It is wrong.
